Empowering the New Era of Developers

The CBIT Developer Student Club (CBIT DSC) is a vibrant and dynamic student-led community at Chaitanya Bharathi Institute of Technology - Hyderabad. Our mission is to cultivate innovation and foster technical skills among students by exploring the latest trends in technology and development.

CBIT DSC organizes a variety of engaging events such as coding competitions, developer bootcamps, interactive workshops, and tech talks. These events are designed to provide students with hands-on experience in domains like app development, web technologies, artificial intelligence, and Competative Coding.
